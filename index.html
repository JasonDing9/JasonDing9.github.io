About

My name is Jason Ding. I graduated with a dual degree in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science and Business Administration from UC Berkeley's Management, Technology, & Entrepreneurship (M.E.T.) program in 2024.

These days I'm most into machine learning and generative models, especially the messy parts where research meets product. Outside of that, I'll happily lose an evening to a hard brain teaser, an Olympiad-style problem, or training an ML model to crack a video game or some other curious problem.

On the work front, I'm at Roblox, building at the intersection of code and product. If you'd like to chat, feel free to email me at jasonding@berkeley.edu.
